📄 PDF Annotator - Save & Restore Demo

This demo shows how to save annotations with author information and restore them later.

📋 How Save & Restore Works:

  • Step 1: Upload a PDF and add annotations
  • Step 2: Click "Save PDF + Annotations" to download TWO files: document.pdf and document-annotations.json
  • Step 3: Later, upload both files using "Load PDF + Annotations"
  • Result: All annotations are restored with the original author information!
